DJI Mavic Pro (Add-On)
Original from 3D Warehouse
Converted to GTA V by b4good   

Features: 

- Working props
- Working lights
- Control like Hydra plane (when you press E, the drone will move forward)

Installing:
1. Put mavic folder inside mods/update/x64/dlcpacks
2. open dlclist.xml inside mods/update/update.rpf/common/data, then add this line

		<Item>dlcpacks:/mavic/</Item>

Done. You're good to fly :D

N.B. To enter the drone, stand above of it

Known bug:
1. When you bail out in the air, the player get stuck in drone, making it explodes
